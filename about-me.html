About Me

Mexican multidisciplinary artist

International recognition for his work in tattoos, also known for the aesthetic and creativity in his work.

With an architecture background and more than 10 years developing his craft in other areas like paint, muralism and sculpture. Roji now finds his work involved with his two latest projects in fashion and toy design, we can see how all his work ties together to form a new vision and graphical proposal.

Despite the short time he has been on the scene, he has shown a strong presence and positioned his work as an authority within the creative industry taking his work to different places like New York, LA, Spain, Italy and around Mexico collaborating with global brands such as Nike, Cadillac, Corona and New Era, as well as internationally recognized musicians like Foo Fighters, Run the Jewels and LP.
